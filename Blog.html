INTRODUCING MORRIS & CO X PINE
The iconic interiors brand has collaborated with PINE and created a collection that will
keep your wardrobe in bloom throughout autumn and winter.





OCTOBER 1, 2018


As the weather cools and our light, bright and breezy clothes are put into storage, we move into autumn with cosy knits, strong tailoring
and romantic dresses on our minds. But colder temps do not at all expel colours, prints and florals from our sartorial lives. It's the
other way around – and PINE has news that lets you stay in bloom all year round. A print collab with the iconic and quintessentially British interiors
brand Morris & Co drops into stores and online on 4 October..




“The Morris & Co prints are some of the most recognised and loved around the world, while William Morris is not only synonymous with the Arts and Crafts movement, but also a strong advocate of beautiful design. We’re thrilled to be collaborating on a collection with this iconic British brand and to be able to share these designs with our customer,” says Pernilla Wohlfahrt, H&M’s Design Director.
